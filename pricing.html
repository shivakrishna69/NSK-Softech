FAQ

Got any questions?

What services does NSK Soft Tech offer?+
NSK Soft Tech offers end-to-end digital services including web development, mobile app development, cybersecurity, digital marketing, cloud architecture, and DevOps. We work with startups, SMBs, and enterprise clients across all industries.
Which IT solution is right for my business?+
The right solution depends on your specific goals, industry, and stage of growth. We always recommend starting with a free 30-minute discovery call where our team can understand your business and propose the most effective approach. Most clients start with a web or mobile project and expand from there.
How much do the services cost?+
Our monthly retainer plans start at $2,499/month for the Starter tier. Project-based engagements typically range from $5,000 for smaller projects to $150,000+ for large enterprise builds. Enterprise clients get custom pricing. We always provide a detailed quote before any work begins — no surprise invoices.
How do I get started with NSK Soft Tech?+
Simply fill out our contact form or email us at hello@nsksofttech.com. We'll schedule a free 30-minute discovery call within 24 hours. After that, we'll send you a detailed proposal with timeline, team composition, and pricing within 3 business days.
Do you sign NDAs and offer IP protection?+
Absolutely. All client work is covered by a Non-Disclosure Agreement. All intellectual property created during the engagement belongs to you. Our contracts are clear on IP ownership, and we offer additional IP escrow arrangements for enterprise clients.
Can I scale my team up or down mid-project?+
Yes. Flexibility is one of our key strengths. You can scale team size up or down with 2-week notice. Growth and Enterprise plan clients can adjust resource allocation monthly based on project needs and priorities.
