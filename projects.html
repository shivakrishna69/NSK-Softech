Flagship Product
Retail · IoT · AI · Computer Vision 2022 – Present
Captana — AI Shelf Monitoring
Captana is a cloud-based, AI-powered shelf monitoring platform that uses computer vision and IoT sensors to help physical retailers maximize on-shelf availability (OSA). Wireless mini-cameras (GDPR-compliant) mount on store shelves and feed real-time visual data to the cloud, where our algorithms detect empty slots, misplaced items, and low-stock situations — automatically triggering replenishment tasks to staff via mobile app. The result: retailers go from 4.5-hour manual gap-scanning processes to just 25 minutes, while significantly increasing unit sales and reducing waste.

SaaS · Memorial Platform · Consumer 2021 – Present
Tributoo — Digital Memorial Platform
Tributoo is an online platform that allows families and friends to create beautiful, personalized digital memorial pages for loved ones who have passed. Fully encrypted and GDPR-compliant, Tributoo lets users share photos, videos, and written memories — privately or publicly. A unique QR code links the digital page to physical memorial cards, grave plaques, and keepsakes, bridging the digital and physical worlds of remembrance. The platform has over 10,000 memorial pages created and is used by families across Europe, America, and Asia. NSK Soft Tech designed and built the entire platform — from subscription system to multi-language support and QR plaque fulfillment.
